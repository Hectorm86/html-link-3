Are you a Gamer?

I've played video games as far back as I can remeber, I was always told they're a waste of time and that they destroy your brain. I never listened to that nonsense and continued gaming on, i'm here to let you know about a few games that have helped shape the gamer I am today.

My Top 3 Favorite Games

  1. Pokemon Red/Blue

    The first game I can ever remember playing and understanding, it was also the the hardest first choice of my life as a kid. I chose Charmander as my starter because that was the coolest of the three at the time. I played nonstop from the minute I got home till it was time to go to sleep. Catching pokemon, leveling up my team and battling all the gym leaders was a true test of my adolescent skills. I'll never forget the feeling of beating all the gym leaders only to find out I still had to battle the 4 most elite trainers of the game, to then beating all 4 after countless strategic attempts, only to then find out I still had one more crucial battle against the games rivaled character who was waiting for me at the end of league. Needless to say i threw my gameboy across the room after losing because I had used all of my healing items. It was a lesson I didnt understand till today, that no matter how far you think you've come, there's always more to challenge and more to achieve in life, don't settle for less than your best always.

  2. Half-Life 2

    This game was released in 2004 for PC and later for other platforms. It is a first-person shooter that continues the story of scientist Gordon Freeman as he fights against an alien invasion on Earth. The game features advanced physics, immersive storytelling, and innovative gameplay mechanics. It received critical acclaim and won numerous Game of the Year awards.

  3. The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

    This game was released in 2015 for PC, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One. It is an open-world action role-playing game that follows the story of Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter known as a Witcher. The game features a rich narrative, complex characters, and a vast world to explore. It received widespread acclaim for its storytelling, gameplay, and graphics, and won several Game of the Year awards.
